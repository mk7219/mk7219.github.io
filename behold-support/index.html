Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change the language?
Behold Bible uses your device's system language. To change the app language, change the language in your device settings. Supported languages: English, German, Spanish.

How do I remove ads?
You can remove all advertisements with a single in-app purchase. Go to the app settings and tap "Remove Ads".

Are my favorites synced across devices?
Favorites are currently stored locally on your device and are not synced across devices.
